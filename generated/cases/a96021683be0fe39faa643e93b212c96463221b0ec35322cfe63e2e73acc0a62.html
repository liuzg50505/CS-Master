Outline of Project:
Taking inspiration from LinkedIn and RatedPeople, the aim of this application is to bridge the
gap in the market regarding short to medium term contract work for web developers.
I am looking to develop a Web Application with a Database of freelance or unemployed Web
Developers, who can be filtered by skills, location, salary, as well as previous work in the
form of an employer review, so that other employers are able to select the best web
developer to suits their needs and requirements.
The target market I am looking to attract regarding employers are small business owners or
hobbyists who are looking to employ a web developer to build them a website from scratch,
or perhaps to enhance their current website.
On the surface, my application will look and feel very streamline, with a focus on being easy
to use. Ideally, my application will provide its users with an uncluttered interface, where they
will not have to navigate through multiple pages of content before they can find what they
are looking for.
5
A Passable Project will:
• Functional Database, with the ability to Create, Read, Update and Delete data.
• User registration and authentication, allowing users to sign up, log in and out.
• Search functionality allowing users to search for developers based on developer
skills, locations, rating and cost.
• Rating system, in which employers can rate the work developers have carried out.
• Functional user profiles, in which developers can provide, detailed information about
themselves, as their areas of expertise, as well as the displaying of ratings from
previous work.
A First Class Project will:
• PayPal integration, allowing employers to pay developers online via PayPal.
• Integrated chat system, allowing both developers and employers to communicate
with one another.
• Enhanced UI/UX design to improve the overall look and feel of the Application for its
users.
• An operational admin login and interface, allowing for website content manipulation
without the need to directly access and edit the database.

ABSTACT
As the internet has become an integral part our everyday lives, where hobbyists and
business owners are more and more looking into creating an online presence, there has
arisen the need for good competent Web Developers. As with any business owner or client,
they want to be provided with a platform that helps them make the best choice when it
comes to the development of a web application for themselves or their business. Therefore,
this report aims to providing an overview of the processes required in the research, design,
development, evaluation, and testing of social media platform for developers, a platform that
aims to fill a gap in the market when it comes to hiring Web Developers.
