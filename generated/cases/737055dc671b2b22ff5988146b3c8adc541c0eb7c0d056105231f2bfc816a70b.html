EDUCATIONAL AIMS
BACK END DEVELOPMENT
4
Subject specific: knowledge, understanding and skills:
The aims of the module are to:
• Develop knowledge and skills necessary for the development
of server-side systems.
• Demonstrate the importance of data management and the
necessary security associated with it.
• Develop an understanding of the need for documented, well-
written, efficient and effective code.
General: knowledge, understanding and skills:
The aims of the module are to:
• Develop critical thinking skills.
• Ensure communication skills (both written and oral) are
appropriate to a defined audience.
LEARNING OUTCOMES
BACK END DEVELOPMENT
5
Subject specific: knowledge, understanding and skills:
On successful completion of this module you will be able to:
1. Apply understanding of the fundamental principles of
server-side development and the associated architectural
components including server, application and database.
2. Explain the role of data storage, access and retrieval in
server-side systems and architectures.
3. Identify and apply appropriate techniques and constructs
to design, implement and test maintainable server-side
systems.
4. Select, test and deploy appropriate back end technologies
and frameworks to develop a solution to a given problem.
General: knowledge, understanding and skills:
On successful completion of this module you will be able to:
5. Schedule work to meet pre-defined deadlines, whilst
taking account of issues that may arise with server
problems.
6. Use justifications to support choices made, ensuring
transferability of knowledge, understanding and skills.
7. Present work in a format and style appropriate to the
audience.
